Description

Our target audience is real estate businesses and independent agents in the market. We also want to be the link between the house builders and the agents so we will focus in the constructors' business as well.

 

Persona

Real Estate Business/Agents

Home builders Business

 

Scenario

  • A sales person is interested in a change of path and our site will answer some of the common questions about Real Estate Market: How to start a career in Real estate? What skills do I need? What is the base salary of an agent?
  • A Real State business wants to expand their inventory. Our site will link them to the best and most reliable Home builders' companies.
  • An independent agent wants more work opportunities. The site have the resources to connect independent agents to Real State companies looking for a temporary worker or Home builder companies wanting to hire a private agent.
  • A home builder company wants to sell their inventory faster. The site will link them to the real state representatives to contact them about marketing strategy, budget, etc.
